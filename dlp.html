Protect your Sensitive Data — Anytime, Anywhere
SecureBiz's Data Loss Prevention (DLP) solutions help organizations monitor, detect, and prevent unauthorized access, leakage, or transfer of sensitive information. Gain visibility and control over your data — whether it’s in use, in motion, or at rest.

Automatically identify, tag, and classify sensitive data to ensure proper protection and compliance.

🌐🚫 Network DLP

Monitor data traffic and prevent leaks across endpoints, emails, cloud storage, and web uploads.

💻🔍 Endpoint DLP
